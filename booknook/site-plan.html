Overview

Purpose

Book Nook is a personal site to share book reviews, track what I'm currently reading, and give quick recommendations to fellow readers. It’s a way to stay motivated to read and connect with others who enjoy good books.

Audience

Book Nook is for casual and dedicated readers, friends, classmates, and family, who are looking for honest book reviews, reading inspiration, and a peek at what I'm reading next.

Dynamic elements

JavaScript will be used to:

  • Render a list of books from an array of book objects
  • Filter books by genre or rating with buttons or a dropdown
  • Display a random “quote of the day” from a book quote array
  • Toggle between “Read” and “Want to Read” lists with conditional styling
